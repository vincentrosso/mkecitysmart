How do I report a parking enforcement sighting?

Open the app, tap the "Report" button on the map or feed screen, select the sighting type (e.g., enforcement spotted, tow truck), and submit. Your report helps other drivers in real time.

How does the parking risk heatmap work?

The heatmap analyzes over 466,000 Milwaukee parking citations to identify high-risk zones. Colors range from green (low risk) to red (high risk). This is a premium feature available with CitySmart Pro.

How do I manage my subscription?

iOS: Go to Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí MKE CitySmart.
Android: Go to Google Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí MKE CitySmart.
You can also manage your subscription from the Profile screen in the app.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Sign in with the same account you used to subscribe, then go to the subscription screen and tap "Restore Purchases."

Why aren't notifications working?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for MKE CitySmart. Also check that location permissions are granted, as some alerts are location-based.
