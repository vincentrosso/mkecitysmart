How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using either of the following methods:

Option A: Delete from within the app

1 Open the MKE CitySmart app on your device.

2 Tap your Profile icon (bottom navigation bar).

3 Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".

4 Confirm by tapping "Yes, Delete My Account" in the confirmation dialog.

5 Your account and data will be scheduled for deletion immediately.

Option B: Request deletion by email

1 Send an email to mkeparkapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request ‚Äî MKE CitySmart".

2 Include the email address associated with your MKE CitySmart account.

3 You will receive a confirmation email within 3 business days.
