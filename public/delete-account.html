Delete Your Account

Account & data deletion

You have the right to delete your MKE CitySmart account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1: In the App

  1. Open MKE CitySmart
  2. Go to Profile (tap your avatar or the profile icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: By Email

Send an email to mkeparkapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

⚠️ What gets deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Vehicle information
  • Saved places and favorites
  • Ticket history and parking data
  • Community reports you've submitted
  • Notification preferences

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To avoid future charges:

  • iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

Cancel your subscription before deleting your account.
