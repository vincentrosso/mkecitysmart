Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

MKE CitySmart ("the App") is developed by Dwayne Sampson ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and optionally your phone number and mailing address.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to provide parking availability, street sweeping alerts, and other location-based features. You can disable location access in your device settings.

Vehicle Information: License plate numbers, vehicle make, model, and color that you voluntarily provide for parking and permit features.

Usage Data: We collect analytics data about how you interact with the App, including screens visited, features used, and crash reports through Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

Community Reports: If you submit parking reports, enforcement sightings, or other community data, that information is shared with other users.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide parking alerts, street sweeping reminders, and garbage pickup notifications
  • Display relevant parking availability near your location
  • Send push notifications for alerts you've enabled
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve the App through analytics and crash reporting
  • Display personalized advertisements (free tier only)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting, and cloud messaging
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • AdMob (Google) — Advertising on the free tier
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In — Authentication providers

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing its use of your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the third-party services listed above to operate the App, and as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see below).

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Disable location tracking via device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings
  • Delete your account entirely via the App or our account deletion page

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase security rules to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mkeparkapp@gmail.com.
